Welcome to the **Nanoparticle Metrology Group** at [Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e)](https://www.tue.nl), part of the [Molecular Biosensing](https://www.tue.nl/en/research/research-groups/molecular-biosensing/) research group.

Our group develops and applies advanced metrological methods for the characterization of nanoparticles in complex biological and environmental matrices. We focus on understanding nanoparticle properties such as size, shape, concentration, and surface chemistry, with applications ranging from nanomedicine to diagnostics.

Explore our website to learn more about our [research](/research/), [team](/people/), [publications and patents](/publications-patents/), and how to [contact us](/contact/).
